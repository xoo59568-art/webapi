How do I change my WhatsApp full profile picture?
▾
Upload your photo on this page, enter your WhatsApp number with country code, get a pair code, and enter it in WhatsApp under Settings → Linked Devices. Your profile picture will be updated automatically to full size.
Is my WhatsApp account safe to use here?
▾
Yes, completely safe. We only link temporarily to change your DP and then immediately log out and delete all session data. We never read your messages or store any credentials.
What is a pair code and why do I need it?
▾
A pair code is WhatsApp's official method to link a device without scanning a QR code. It's the same technology used by WhatsApp Web. We use it to temporarily access your account just to change your profile picture.
How long does the process take?

The entire process takes less than 60 seconds. Upload your photo, enter your number, receive the code instantly, enter it in WhatsApp, and your profile picture is changed — all done!

What happens to my photo after the process?

Your photo is automatically deleted from our servers immediately after your profile picture is set. We do not store, share, or use your photos for any other purpose.
